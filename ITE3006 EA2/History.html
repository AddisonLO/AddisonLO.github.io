History of Hinatazaka46

The history of hinatazaka46 is quite complicated so I will sperate them into two part.

As a result, management company decide to create a "Under"(spare)group for her.

    May 2016, management company announced the news of hiring the new Hiragana keyakizaka46 members.After the audition, there were eleven new members joined this new group, called "1st generation". However, after the fans knew about them and saw their face, they critisized that they were not beautiful enough and think this new group was screwed. Spare group seemed to be not important anymore so their resource(chance of release new album and singles,have appearance on variety show etc.) was keep reducing. They start to introspect if they should quit the group as soon as possible.At Augest 2017, new members joined the group, called "2nd generation". 1st generation thought they were being abondoned so they locked themself in a room. They eventually came out because the show is going to start and staffs consolate for a long time.

Finally there was a chance for them, the Ace of keyakizaka46, Hirate Yurina absent the shows because she was sick but other member can't dance like her(It's sound ridiculous but it's real)so the show artist become Hiragana Keyakizaka46. All seat were full in this three day show and prove their popularity. Their road seemed to be successfull, they ovwn a variety show.
