Basic info

This is a fanpage about a japanese idol group called Hinatazaka46(日向坂４６). Founded since September 2015. This idol group was the subgroup of Keyakizaka46(欅坂４６) and called hiragana keyakizaka 46 　　(けやき坂４６）. At February 2019, its Management company decide to sperate this idol group from Keyakizaka46, rename it to Hinatazaka46. Fan group named "Ohisama"
