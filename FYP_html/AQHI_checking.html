Why is AQHI important for personal health?


Ans: AQHI provides a measure of the potential health risks associated with exposure to air pollution and it will provide a single number that represents the overall air quality and associated health risk.



What happens if we expose ourselves to air pollutants for a long time?

Some problems will occur:


1.Respiratory problems: Long-term exposure to air pollutants, such as fine particulate matter and nitrogen dioxide, can cause respiratory problems such as chronic bronchitis, asthma, and reduced lung function


2.Cardiovascular problems: Air pollution exposure has been linked to an increased risk of cardiovascular problems, such as heart attacks and strokes because the pollutants can enter the bloodstream and cause inflammation and damage to the heart and blood vessels.


3.Cancer: Exposure to certain air pollutants, such as benzene and polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons, has been linked to an increased risk of cancer.
